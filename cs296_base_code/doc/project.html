This is final design of Shrimp Rover Bot,an All Terrain Bot consisting of three parts mainly the front fork, the bogeys in the middle and the back fork. Front fork is the most critical part of the bot as it helps in climbing over the surface.
This bot can get over the wall of height upto twice the diameter of its tyre.
This bot can get into cliffs and holes and can come out easily.
